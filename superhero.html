Ironman

About Ironman

Ironman is a fictional American superhero created by Marvel Studios. He is awesome and works for Stark Industries, but later breaks away and decides to protect the world. He dies in Avengers Endgame.

Ironman getting ready to snap his fingers

Ironman's characteristics

  1. Tony Stark is very honest.
  2. He can communicate to others in full detail even during times of stress.
  3. He is very confident and proud of what he has done.
  4. He can be very humorous at times.
